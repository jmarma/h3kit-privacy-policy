1. Introduction

H3Kit ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application developed by H3ight LLC that helps rope access professionals track and manage their safety equipment, inspections, and compliance records. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using H3Kit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (name, email address, password)
  • Update your profile information
  • Contact us for support
  • Provide feedback or participate in surveys

2.2 Equipment and Inspection Data

To provide our core services, we collect and store:

  • Gear inventory information (equipment names, types, serial numbers, purchase dates)
  • Inspection records (dates, results, notes, photos)
  • Job information (client names, dates, locations, hours worked)
  • Compliance reports and documentation

2.3 Photos and Media

With your permission, we access your device's camera and photo library to:

  • Take photos of equipment during inspections
  • Attach existing photos to inspection records
  • Document equipment condition and compliance

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:

You can update your personal information at any time through the app's profile settings. You can also modify or delete gear and inspection records as needed.

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data. To do so:

  1. Contact us at work@h3ight.com
  2. Specify that you want your account deleted
  3. We will process your request within 30 days

You can opt out of promotional emails by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

Data Type Retention Period
Account Information Until account deletion or 2 years of inactivity

8. Children's Privacy

H3Kit is intended for use by rope access professionals and is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately at work@h3ight.com.

9. International Data Transfers

H3Kit is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

By using H3Kit, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

We take appropriate measures to ensure that your information receives an adequate level of protection in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.
